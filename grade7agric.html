UNIT 5: WATER

Objectives

After this course student will be able to:

  • Illustrate the water cycle.
  • describe the water cycle.
  • identify ways of controlling water pollution

Introduction

In grade 6 we defined water pollution and outlined and outlined the causes and effects ofwatere pollution. In this unit we are going to didcribe the water cycle,outling its maincomponents. The unit will also cover ways of controling the problem of water pollution.

A cell consists of three parts: the cell membrane, the nucleus, and, between the two, the cytoplasm. Within the cytoplasm lie intricate arrangements of fine fibers and hundreds or even thousands of miniscule but distinct structures called organelles.
